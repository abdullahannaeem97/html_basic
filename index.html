Watch the Sunset

What a wonderful way to end your day at the beach! Florida's sunsets are spectacular, particularly from the
vantage point of any Gulf Coast beach. There are two beach communities that think every sunset is reason
enough to celebrate, Clearwater Beach and Key West. Those communities put on a nightly party that includes
live entertainment, street performers, and craft vendors.
